About Us

The Rock of Untouchable Ministry is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community dedicated to empowering lives through divine guidance and prophetic insight. Founded on the unshakeable foundation of God's Word, our ministry serves as a beacon of hope, transformation, and spiritual growth.

Our mission is to equip believers with the tools and understanding necessary to walk confidently in their God-given purpose. Through powerful worship, anointed teaching, and prophetic ministry, we strive to create an atmosphere where individuals can encounter the living God and experience His transformative power.

At The Rock of Untouchable Ministry, we believe in the active presence of the Holy Spirit and the continuation of all spiritual gifts in the modern church. Our leadership team is committed to fostering an environment of love, acceptance, and spiritual maturity, where every member can grow in their faith and discover their unique calling in Christ.
